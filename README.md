Sakila Database Analysis
SQL business analysis of DVD rental operations, uncovering 15-20% revenue opportunities through inventory optimization and VIP customer strategies.

The Challenge
Analyzed the Sakila database (1,000 films, 16K rentals, 599 customers) to identify why high-demand categories like Action & Sci-Fi were under-stocked while Foreign films tied up capital with weak rental performance.
​
Results Delivered
(1) Supply-Demand Gap Analysis: Identified $ tied up in low-performing Foreign category inventory
(2) VIP Retention Strategy: Top 10 customers contribute 10-15x average value
(3) Hit Film Identification: 3-4 copies/store of top performers across diverse categories
